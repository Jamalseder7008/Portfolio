The Path

September 28, 2025 3–4 min read

Read—and the merciful doors of your Lord will open. A path to wonderful stories to come and their magnificent beauty. Many adventures that a soul will embark on wherein it will learn the truth—the truth of its ultimate reality.

This reality must be realized as an ever-evolving subject. Why a subject? Because it must submit to continue on the correct path. Without submission there is no movement; the path becomes a place. Look back, smile, and reflect. There is light, and there is more beauty. Now look ahead and move toward what is brighter. We are still on that same path, yet through reflection the path is refined. This is called building an intention.

Intentions shape the space of the straight path. It does not curve nor become obscure; it is painted with a beauty unlike any other. There are stars in the distance—some within sight, yet out of reach. Within the stars are flashes of moments. Are these our memories to come or memories of the past? Perhaps a mixture of both. We have avoided most memories save two: one of love and one of a path—motherly love and the white path.

Those glorious doors we have just entered are the doors of the heart and mind—one in conjunction. We could separate them, yet let us keep them merged with the intention of unity. Unity builds the reality that our subject continues upon until completion. Love is the idea we desire to complete. Why is love such a powerful feeling? It is spoken of by many, yet no school has an algorithm to produce it. Rather, love is learned through experience.

There is a type of love that is deeply fulfilling and other types that satisfy only to an extent. Two helpful lenses:

  • Conditional Love — a love arising with events and reliance upon a person or circumstance.
  • Intrinsic Love — an inner, resilient love that does not diminish when conditions change.

Love need not be divided; it is one light refracted through different moments. Each aspect can be nurtured, like a flower blooming until its beauty is revealed. Conditional love accompanies unfolding events. Intrinsic love abides—regardless of what happens, the love exists among us. To walk the path is to let intention polish both, so that love orients us toward the One who placed the path beneath our feet.
