Online Audit Scheduler

In order to schedule an event, we must discover the time we want to hold the event without obstructing on other events and at the same moment, displaying that event in a way where users can uncover the necessary information. Here we have a clear problem that must be solved; specifically a constraint satisfaction problem, CSP. My team will work together to develop a working prototype that can be applied to scheduling audits within our University, able to be used by professors in a simple manner. This project will require the use of Front-end, Back-end, and Artificial Intelligence algorithms to construct a full-stack application.
